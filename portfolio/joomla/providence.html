Our Lady of Providence Junior-Senior High School is a college and life prep school located in historic Clarksville, Indiana.

The American Bishops Pastoral “To Teach as Jesus Did” expanded the dimensions of Catholic school when they stated, “The educational mission of the Church is an integrating ministry embracing three interlocking dimensions: the message revealed by God which the Church proclaims; fellowship in the life of the Holy Spirit; and, service to the Christian community and the entire human community.”
